NETGEAR Downloader Settings help

Maximum Download Speed (0 means no limit)

This setting limits your maximum HTTP/FTP/BT download speed (the unit is KB/s). The default setting is 0 (0 means there is no limit to your download speed).

Maximum Upload Speed (0 means no limit)

This setting limits your maximum HTTP/FTP/BT upload speed (the unit is KB/s). The default setting is 0 (0 means there is no limit to your upload speed).

Maximum Download Task

This setting configures your maximum download task of the NETGEAR Downloader for the wireless router. The default setting is based on the wireless router CPU computing power and DRAM size.

Allow the system to manage your NETGEAR Downloader automatically

This option allows the system to manage your NETGEAR Downloader. When the box is checked, the system removes all the completed download tasks that are listed in the download task information table, so that these completed tasks do not occupy your upload bandwidth. If the box is not checked (default setting), the system does nothing, even if the download tasks are completed. In this case, your completed download files will still be shared with other clients.
