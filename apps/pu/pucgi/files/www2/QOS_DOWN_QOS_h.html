DownStream QoS Settings

This is a QoS function specifically designed for improving online video streaming quality. When you have heavy Internet downloading traffic, the online video streaming quality will be easily affected. With this feature enabled, the system automatically detects the video streaming and prioritize the video streaming traffic as high priority. So the online video streaming quality will not easily impacted by background downloading traffic.
